Managing Kubernetes Workload the Easy Way!
{{- with .Site.GetPage "section" "documentation/020_getting-started" -}} Get Started {{- end -}}

Get your Workload deployed consistently throughout your Kubernetes Landscape.

Kubernetes-Native
Kubernetes is the de-facto standard for managing modern container-based cloud applications. With Potter on top of your Gardener Landscape, you can finally start managing your Kubernetes Workload as its meant to be - central, declarative and based on Open Standards.
Leveraging Kubernetes Standards
Whatever you need to deploy to your K8s Clusters, Potter has you covered, as it currently already supports Helm Charts and KAPP-based deployment packages (with more to come).
Open Community Effort
The Potter project is open for collaborators and adopters. It aims at delivering a standard workload management solution that meets the needs of the K8s community and ecosystem.
